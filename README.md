## Rules --> 
  ```
    $ 10 available equipment
  
    $ Each team with its own life
   
    $ Each team with its own damage
      
    $ 50 energy per round
        
    $ 1 attack = 1 energy
      
    $ 1 defense = 2 energy
     
    $ The last survivor wins
    
  ```
